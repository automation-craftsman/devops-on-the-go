[--message-begins--] DevOps combines software development and IT operations to improve collaboration and streamline the software delivery process. It focuses on automation, continuous integration, and continuous delivery (CI/CD) to enable faster and more reliable releases. By using tools like Jenkins, Docker, and Kubernetes, DevOps enhances efficiency and reduces manual errors. The goal is to foster a culture of continuous improvement and rapid response to customer needs. [--message-ends--]
